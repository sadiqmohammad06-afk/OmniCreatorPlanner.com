    Frequently Asked Questions

    How are earnings calculated? +

    Estimates use selected platform, views, short-form views, country, niche, creator tier, sponsorship deals and public benchmark RPM ranges.

    Are these figures guaranteed? +

    No. Actual earnings may vary based on audience location, engagement, watch time, monetization status, platform policy and advertiser demand.

    Can I use this for brand proposals? +

    Yes, this tool can help create rough planning estimates, but final brand pricing should be negotiated separately.

    Is my data stored? +

    For this demo, calculations run in your browser and no account is required. Theme and cookie choices may be saved locally in your browser.
